### Key Findings

- Identified two distinct calibration transfer failure modes.
- Demonstrated >80–90% RMSE reduction on B5 using few-shot adaptation.
- Showed persistent high-concentration compression on B1.
- Verified superiority of physics-informed features over raw features.
- Found that simple mean/std alignment outperformed more complex correction methods under limited calibration budgets.
